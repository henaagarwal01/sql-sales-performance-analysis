# 📊 Key Insights

- New York City generates the highest revenue and profit
- Philadelphia has high sales but is overall loss-making
- Discounts above 30% lead to significant losses
- Tables and Bookcases are major loss-making sub-categories
- West region performs best in sales and profit
- Some cities have strong sales but poor profitability

# 💡 Recommendations

- Avoid high discounts (>30%)
- Focus on profitable cities (New York, Los Angeles, Seattle)
- Improve pricing strategy for loss-making products
- Investigate loss-making cities like Philadelphia and Houston
- Optimize product mix for better margins
